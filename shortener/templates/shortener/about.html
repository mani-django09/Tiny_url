Our Journey

The TinyURL.run Story

TinyURL.run began as a solution to a common problem in the digital age: the inefficiency of sharing long URLs across platforms.

🚀 Our Beginning

In early 2023, our founding team of web developers and digital marketers recognized a gap in the market. While URL shorteners existed, they lacked the comprehensive analytics, reliability, and user-friendly features needed by modern businesses and individual users.

🌱 Growth & Evolution

Within our first quarter, we surpassed 100,000 users and processed over 1 million shortened URLs. Listening to our users, we quickly expanded our feature set to include custom domains, QR code generation, and advanced click analytics.

🌍 Where We Are Today

Today, TinyURL.run serves over 500,000 users worldwide, from social media managers and digital marketers to casual web users looking for an easier way to share links. We've maintained our commitment to a free core service while expanding our technology to ensure industry-leading reliability and security.
